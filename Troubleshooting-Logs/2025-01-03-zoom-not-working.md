Troubleshooting Log 07 — Zoom Microphone Not Working

User: Executive
Criticality: High

Performed
Checked Windows sound input
Disabled “Allow apps to take exclusive control”
Updated audio driver
Reinstalled Zoom
Mic worked on Teams but not on Zoom → isolated to Zoom app

Root Cause
Automatic noise cancellation disabled mic completely on old hardware.

Fix
Enabled legacy audio mode.
