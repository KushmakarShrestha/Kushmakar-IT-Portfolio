Troubleshooting-log-22.md

Issue: Laptop overheating and shutting down.
Impact: High

Diagnosis
CPU temp > 95°C
Fan clogged with dust
Background processes: Adobe Updater eating CPU

Actions
Cleaned fan/vents using compressed air.
Removed unnecessary Adobe startup apps.
Updated BIOS and thermal management drivers.
Enabled "Balanced" mode instead of "Performance".

Resolution
Temp dropped to 65–70°C under load.

Root Cause
Dust blockage + runaway process.
